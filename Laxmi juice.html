Laxmi juice:

Lot of time I had tasted yummy "packed lunch"from laxmi juice but first time got a chance to visit the joint. In the crowed market near to bus stand, this is a popular 2 story joint serving fast food & snacks on 1st floor & thali food on the 2nd floor. I found the following USP :- reasonably good Food , good variety to serve, hygienic environment, a good ambience, quick service & very pocket friendly price. Not had a chance to visit 2nd floor.. May be visiting soon..
